# Course 1 Preflop (PPWM) — A7: 30-Day Preflop Plan (Habits > Heroics)

## The core idea
Preflop shows up every hand, so tiny improvements compound.
MTT Poker School’s “10-minute training plan” is a simple framework based on *purposeful practice*:
- Don’t try to memorize everything.
- Train one specific spot, with feedback, and measurable goals.  
Source: https://www.mttpokerschool.com/single-post/otb-050-the-10-minute-training-plan-to-fix-your-preflop-leaks

It references purposeful practice elements:
- **Focus**
- **Feedback**
- **Clearly defined goals**  
Source: https://www.mttpokerschool.com/single-post/otb-050-the-10-minute-training-plan-to-fix-your-preflop-leaks

## Default daily session (10 minutes)
MTT Poker School’s suggested split:
1. **Study the range first** and “bucket hands together” — 3 minutes
2. **Drill 100 hands** in that specific spot — 4 minutes
3. **Review mistakes and make notes** — 3 minutes  
Source: https://www.mttpokerschool.com/single-post/otb-050-the-10-minute-training-plan-to-fix-your-preflop-leaks

## 30-day plan (mapped to A1–A6 topics)
### Week 1 (Days 1–7): RFI fundamentals
- Day 1–3: BTN RFI at one stack depth (e.g., 30bb or 75bb)
- Day 4–5: CO RFI
- Day 6: HJ/LJ RFI
- Day 7: Review: write “bottom-of-range” cutoff hands for each position

(Use PokerCoaching chart page as reference for position-by-position opens and consistent sizing.)  
Source: https://pokercoaching.com/preflop-charts

### Week 2 (Days 8–14): 3-bets
- Days 8–10: IP 3-bet spots (BTN vs CO/HJ)
- Days 11–13: OOP 3-bet spots (SB vs BTN/CO)
- Day 14: Sizing + notes (IP ~3x, OOP bigger as a baseline)  
Source (IP/OOP sizing baseline): https://upswingpoker.com/3-bet-strategy-aggressive-preflop/

### Week 3 (Days 15–21): Facing 3-bets (defense)
- Days 15–17: Call vs 3-bet in position (suited broadways, suited aces, suited connectors)
- Days 18–20: OOP defense tightening (cut dominated offsuit broadways)
- Day 21: Opponent adjustments (tight vs aggressive 3-bettors)  
Source: https://pokercoaching.com/blog/3-bet-calling-ranges/

### Week 4 (Days 22–30): Blinds + ISO + stack depth integration
- Days 22–24: BB defense heads-up (pot odds discipline)
- Days 25–26: BB defense multiway: **tighten/select hands that realize**  
  Source: https://pokercoaching.com/blog/defending-the-big-blind-multiway/
- Days 27–28: ISO raising sizing + tighter-than-RFI range concept  
  Sources:
  - https://www.888poker.com/magazine/improve-iso-raising-poker-strategy
  - https://smartpokerstudy.com/how-to-target-limpers-for-fun-and-profits/
- Days 29–30: Stack depth review (shallow vs deep hand class changes)  
  Source: https://pokercoaching.com/blog/preflop-strategy-guide-mastering-position-and-stack-depth/

## Tracking (make it measurable)
- Create a simple scorecard per spot:
  - Accuracy % (correct action)
  - Biggest 3 recurring mistakes
  - 1 “rule” you’re adopting next session
