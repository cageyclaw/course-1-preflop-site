# Course 1 Preflop (PPWM) — A2: 3-bet Strategy

## What a 3-bet is
- A **3-bet** is most commonly the **re-raise versus a preflop open** (then 4-bet, 5-bet, etc.).  
  Source: Upswing Poker 3-bet strategy article  
  https://upswingpoker.com/3-bet-strategy-aggressive-preflop/

## Why we 3-bet (value + pressure)
Upswing frames two big goals that map cleanly to preflop 3-betting:
- **Build pots with hands likely to win at showdown** (value)
- **Win pots without showdown / deny equity** with hands that don’t want to just call (semi-bluffs)  
Source: https://upswingpoker.com/3-bet-strategy-aggressive-preflop/

## Range construction: Linear vs Polar
Upswing’s clean definitions (usable in course copy):
- **Linear 3-bet range**: “premium hands, strong hands, and decent hands that benefit from protection.”
- **Polarized 3-bet range**: “premium hands and semi-bluffs.”  
Source: https://upswingpoker.com/3-bet-strategy-aggressive-preflop/

### When to use which (teaching rule)
- **Linear** when you expect **calls** (open-raiser is sticky; players behind can squeeze; you want equity + playability).
- **Polar** when you expect **folds**, especially **in position**, and want to attack with blockers / hands that play okay when called.

## Default 3-bet sizing (simple baseline)
- Upswing baseline for 100bb (non-ante) games:
  - **In position (IP): ~3x** the open size
  - **Out of position (OOP): ~4–4.5x** the open size  
  Source: https://upswingpoker.com/3-bet-strategy-aggressive-preflop/

PokerCoaching also publishes a sizing heuristic on its chart page for tournament exploitative opens/3-bets:
- “**3x the raise when 3-betting IP**” and “**3.5x the raise when 3-betting OOP**” (as a listed guideline alongside opens/4-bets).  
  Source: https://pokercoaching.com/preflop-charts

**How to present this**: “Pick one system and stick to it; don’t size based on hand strength.”

## Key mistakes to warn about
- **3-betting too merged OOP vs tight opens** (you build big pots with dominated hands).
- **Never 3-bet bluffing** (opponents can overfold everything but premiums).
- **Sizing tells** (big with value, small with bluffs).
