# D2 — 3-bet or Defend Lab (vs Open)

**Goal:** Choose between **3-bet / call / fold** vs an open, using a clean micros baseline.

**Sizing defaults (PokerCoaching-style):**
- **IP 3-bet:** 3x the open
- **OOP 3-bet:** 3.5x the open

**Scoring (stoplight):**
- Green = optimal, Yellow = acceptable, Red = mistake

> Educational content only. No guarantees. Results vary.

## Coaching themes
- SB: **3-bet/fold** more, flat less.
- Don’t defend dominated offsuit hands “because pot odds.”
- Value-heavy 3-bets print vs wide opens.
