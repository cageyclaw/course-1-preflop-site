# D1 — RFI Decision Lab (Open or Fold)

**Goal:** Build automatic, position-aware RFI discipline (rake-aware).  
**Format:** 30 hands. Decide **Open** or **Fold** as first-in.  
**Default stack:** ~100bb online cash.  
**Sizing default:** 2.5x open (keep it consistent).  

**Scoring (stoplight):**
- **Green (2 pts):** Optimal
- **Yellow (1 pt):** Acceptable but not best
- **Red (0 pts):** Mistake

**Total:** 60 points.

> Educational content only. No guarantees. Results vary.

---

## Hands (30)
For each item: choose **Open** or **Fold**.

1) **UTG:** A♠ Q♦ → **Open (Green)** — Strong broadway; plays well vs 3-bets.
2) **UTG:** A♦ 9♠ → **Fold (Green)** — Dominated Axs/off-suit problems in EP.
3) **UTG:** K♠ Q♠ → **Open (Green)** — Strong suited broadway.
4) **UTG:** Q♣ J♦ → **Fold (Yellow)** — Borderline; many micros pools punish EP looseness.

9) **HJ:** 5♠ 5♥ → **Open (Green)** — Pair is fine.
10) **HJ:** J♠ 9♠ → **Open (Yellow)** — Suited connector-ish; fine if blinds aren’t aggro.

11) **CO:** A♦ T♦ → **Open (Green)** — Value-heavy late-position open.

## Coaching themes
- EP mistakes are expensive (rake + OOP + 3-bet pressure).
- BTN wins by volume and position, not by adding offsuit trash.
- SB should be tighter than BTN and avoid marginal OOP continues.

## Next step
If you scored <45/60, tighten EP/SB and rerun this drill in 48 hours.
